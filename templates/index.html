Execute Commands

In the C.Y.R.U.S. system, you can execute terminal commands using your voice. By pressing the record button and speaking, your voice is transcribed to text, which is then processed and executed as a command in the terminal.

record_voice_over

Vocal Conversation

To have a conversation with C.Y.R.U.S., you need to press the record button on the user interface, which starts recording your voice. After you finish speaking, release the button and CYRUS will process your voice command, and respond in a conversation like manner.

Gesture Recognition

swipe_up

Gesture recognition is used to identify specific hand movements, which are then mapped to functions that control light settings, such as toggling the light on/off or changing its color. This allows you to interact with the program and control its behavior using hand gestures.
